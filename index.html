ABOUT:

After being bitten by a genetically modified spider in a scientific demonstration, young nerd Peter Parker gains superpowers. Initially, he intends to use them to make money, adopting the name Spider-Man and performing in exhibition matches. However, when witnessing the murder of his uncle Ben and feeling guilty, Peter decides no longer to use his powers for his own gain but to face evil, having as his first great challenge the psychotic Green Goblin.

Peter Parker is trying to find out more about his origins. He finds a folder that once belonged to his father and wants to find out why his parents disappeared. His search leads him to Oscorp and Dr. Curt Connors, whose alter ego is the lethal Lizard.

After fighting alongside the Avengers, it's time for young Peter Parker to return home to New York alongside his Aunt May. Under the watchful eye of mentor Tony Stark, Peter begins to embrace his new identity as Spider-Man and fights petty crimes in the surrounding area daily. He believes he has found his life's mission when the terrifying villain Vulture emerges threatening the city. The problem is that the task will not be as easy as he imagined.
